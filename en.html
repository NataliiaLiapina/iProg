about iProg+


Module (script) for iProg + for working with Delphi HV240\MR140 blocks in BSL mode. The device was developed as a specialized programmer for working with various automotive systems.

It is intended, first of all, for lifting the "sleeping" blocks in case of unsuccessful firmware via OBD.

Side "properties" of this module include the ability to write a non-trained EEPROM.

Allows you to install the ECU on the car and operate it. If necessary, bind the keys, having reached the master on their own.

The module reads / writes EEPROM and FLASH without counting CS. Does not carry out identification and does not work with calibrations.
